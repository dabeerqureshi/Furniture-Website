Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long does delivery take?

We offer same-day delivery in most areas for orders placed before 2 PM. Standard delivery takes 1-3 business days.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes, we provide professional assembly services for an additional fee. This can be arranged at the time of order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit cards, and digital payments. All payment methods are secure and convenient.

Can I return or exchange furniture?

Yes, we offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Please contact our customer service for return instructions.
